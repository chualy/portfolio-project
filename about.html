Why Front-end Development?

My schooling days were in the era when we had internet dial-ups, and we connected with friends using Friendster and MSN Messenger. Many of us also maintain a blog. Back then, I only knew how to copy HTML snippets to change my blog skin.

Times have change and the internet as well as mobile apps are now part of our everyday life.

Now, as I slowly learn and work with HTML, CSS and Javascript, it is interesting to know how much effort is spent in these web apps and pages we consume daily.

I enjoy combining art and science to produce web pages to share information. I am thrilled to be able to explore my creativity and make these pages work and look good.
